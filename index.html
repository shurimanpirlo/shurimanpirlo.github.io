About

I am a proficient 3D artist, specializing in freelance work. With over six years of dedicated experience, I have honed my expertise in the use of Blender. Additionally, I possess advanced skills in Photoshop and Illustrator.

My professional strengths lie in hard surface modeling and product rendering, primarily geared towards creating captivating visuals for advertising and marketing campaigns. Furthermore, my background extends to photography, logo design, and brand identity development.

My deep-seated passion for art and creativity is a driving force in my work, and I maintain a steadfast commitment to artistic expression across diverse mediums.
